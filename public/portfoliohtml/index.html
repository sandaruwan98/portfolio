Li-Fix

Li-fix is a comprehensive lamp post management system that was developed to streamline the maintenance and inventory management of lamp posts in the Baddegama Divisional Secretariat area. Each lamp post is assigned a unique identification number, and the system keeps track of the precise locations of all lamp posts. Li-fix is designed to detect and manage any anomalies in inventory, making it easy for technicians, clerks, storekeepers, and the Divisional Secretary to keep track of the lamp post repairing complaints in their area. This is the second-year group project of the university. Despite the challenge of not using any frameworks, this project has achieved its goal of creating an efficient, easy-to-use lamp post management system.

Secure QR Scanner

Secure QR Scanner is a mobile application that provides a secure way to scan QR codes and protect users from malicious URLs. This app utilizes the SafetyNet Safe Browsing API to check for and identify any potentially harmful links contained within QR codes. Additionally, registered users can contribute to the app's database by reporting and adding any malicious websites they come across. With a user-friendly interface and robust security features, Secure QR Scanner is a valuable tool for anyone looking to protect themselves from cyber threats while utilizing the convenience of QR code technology.
